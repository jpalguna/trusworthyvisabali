Familly KITAS

What is familly KITAS?

  • If you are married with an Indonesian citizen outside Indonesia, or in Indonesia, or children/husband or wife from The KITAS holder you want to apply for a spouse KITAS or dependent KITAS to live in Indonesia this Visa/Kitas is the multiple entry visa that Valid for 1 year.

Document requirements for Married Kitas

  • Marriage certificate.
  • Bank statement (min. 2,000 USD)
  • CNI (Certificate of No Impediment) letter. You will get it from your countryâ€™s embassy in Indonesia.
  • Family Card (Kartu Keluarga) of the Indonesian spouse.
  • ID (KTP) of the Indonesian spouse.
  • Birth certificate (Akta Lahir) of the Indonesian spouse.
  • Your Passport with minimum 18 months validity.
  • Domicile letter from sub-district (kantor kecamatan) or domicile letter from Building Management.

Document requirements for Dependent Kitas (Wife/ Husband holding Independent Kitas)

  • Marriage certificate.
  • Bank statement (min. 2,000 USD) for both of Husband and Wife.
  • E- KITAS from the KITAS holder ( only for children/wife/ husband that apply for dependent kitas).
  • PMA BUSINESS LICENSE from the kitas holder ( Only for children/wife/husband that apply for dependent kitas)Documents For The Spouse KITAS C317.
  • Photograph half body with blue, white or red background.
  • Passport with minimum validity 18 months.
  • Vaccine certificate minimum 2 doses.
  • Scan passport from the Indpendent KITAS holder ( only for children/wife/ husband that apply for dependent kitas).

Document requirements for Dependent Kitas for Children Below 17 Years Old (Parents holding Independent Kitas)

  • Marriage certificate ( If Parents Married), If not, you should provide the letter of living together from Your Government or Indonesian Government.
  • Bank statement (min. 2,000 USD) for both of Husband and Wife.
  • E- KITAS from the KITAS holder ( only for children/wife/ husband that apply for dependent kitas).
  • PMA BUSINESS LICENSE from the kitas holder ( Only for children/wife/husband that apply for dependent kitas)Documents For The Spouse KITAS C317.
  • Photograph half body with blue, white or red background.
  • Passport with minimum validity 18 months.
  • Scan passport from the Indpendent KITAS holder ( only for children/wife/ husband that apply for dependent kitas).
  • Children birth certificate.

How to apply familly KITAS

  • Click the link to connect with our whatsapp or email.
  • Send all of the document of requirements by whatsapp or email.
  • We will give you the Invoice to make the payment. After you make the Payment, we will start the process directly.
  • We will start the process by register your company sponsorship to Directorat Immigration Office Jakarta and it takes approximately around 2 working days process until The Government approve your company as a sponsorship.
  • After The Government approve your sponsorship, We will do the submission of your Dependent or Married Visa to immigration office Jakarta.
  • After the E- Visa issued, we will send it through your whatsapp or email.

Note :

  • Please kindly wait for the process until the E-Visa issued and do not enter Indonesia without the E- Visa.
  • The process of the E-Visa is based on the working days of Indonesian Government, so, it will not Include weekend and public holiday.
  • When the E- Visa already issued, please kindly enter Indonesia before 90 days since the E-Visa issued or the visa will be canceled automatically.
  • After you arrived in Indonesia, please kindly send the sticker or stamp of arrival through our whatsapp or email.
  • Please Kindly give your passport to us within 30 days since your arrival in Indonesia because we need to report it to immigration to issue your multiple entry.
  • You have to visit immigration office in Bali one time for photo and fingerprint. We will inform you once the schedule already issue by Immigration Office.

Price :
