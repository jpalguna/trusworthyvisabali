Investor KITAS

What is investor KITAS?

  • Investor KITAS is a Limited Stay Permit to an Investor to stay and be in the Territory of Indonesia for stay period one years (KITAS C313) or two years (KITAS C314).
  • Residence Permits that have been granted to Investors can be converted into Permanent Stay Permits (KITAP).
  • Investor KITAS giving you multiple entry permit. Investor KITAS is the most comfort and efficient stay permit for permanent stay in Indonesia.

Document requirements

    Copies of necessary documents from the sponsoring company

  • Notary Deed.
  • Deed of Establishment from Ministry of Law and Human Rights.
  • A company's NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha) is its official identification number.
  • Operational License or Standard Certificate From the OSS.
  • Company Tax ID (NPWP Badan Usaha).
  • All of the OSS License.
  • ID of Indonesian Staff.

    Requirement for Foreigner ( Applicants)

  • passport must be valid for at least 18 months to applying 1 Year Investor Kitas.
  • A proof of Bank Statement with minimum balance at least 2,000 USD or equivalent.
  • Photograph Half Body with Blue, Red or White Background.
  • Photograph Half Body with Blue, Red or White Background.
  • Vaccine Certificate Minimum 2 doses.
  • Address of place of stay for living in Bali.

How to apply investor KITAS

  • Click the link to connect with our whatsapp or email.
  • Send all of the document of requirements by whatsapp or email.
  • We will give you the Invoice to make the payment. After you make the Payment, we will start the process directly.
  • We will start the process by register your company sponsorship to Directorat Immigration Office Jakarta and it takes approximately around 2 working days process until The Government approve your company as a sponsorship.
  • After The Government approve your company as a sponsorship, We will do the submission of your Investor Kitas or C314 Visa to immigration office Jakarta.
  • After the E- Visa issued, we will send it through your whatsapp or email.

Note

  • Please kindly wait for the process until the E-Visa issued and do not enter Indonesia without the E- Visa.
  • The process of the E-Visa is based on the working days of Indonesian Government, so, it will not Include weekend and public holiday.
  • When the E- Visa already issued, please kindly enter Indonesia before 90 days since the E-Visa issued or the visa will be canceled automatically.
  • After you arrived in Indonesia, please kindly send the sticker or stamp of arrival through our whatsapp or email.
  • Please Kindly give your passport to us within 30 days since your arrival in Indonesia because we need to report it to immigration to issue your multiple entry.
  • You have to visit immigration office in Bali one time for photo and fingerprint. We will inform you once the schedule already issue by Immigration Office.
