VoA Extension

What is VoA?

  • Visa on Arrival (VoA) is a temporary entry permit issued by the Indonesian Government to foreign citizens.Unlike other visas, VoA provides convenience by bypassing complicated bureaucracy. Foreign nationals can directly apply for it upon arrival at the destination airport, as long as they have the necessary documents.
  • VoA can be used for social visits, business purposes, or vacations. Extending the VoA is straightforward, especially with the introduction of e-VoA, which enables online applications and extensions.

Document requrirements

  • Original Passport.

How to apply VoA extension

  • Click the link to connect with our whatsapp or email.
  • Send the stamp visa that you get from the airport and we will explain it by whatsapp or email.
  • On chosen date and period of time, courier will come and pickup your passport.
  • Courier will go directly to Denpasar or Jimbaran, where he will give your passport to the officer.
  • From that day you shouldn't leave far from main area of Bali because any day you might be asked to come for biometric and fingerprint but if you plan to travelling around Indonesia during the extension, we can discuss about it on whatsapp or email.
  • You will go on that date to the pointed Immigration Office. You will have a number which you will show in counter and officer will direct you to the room where your fingerprints will be scanned and face photo taken. (Please mind to come early morning, wear long pants and cover your arms in the Immigration office).

Note :

  • Your passport will be in Immigration Office since you apply until extension process is finished.
  • You have to be available to go for biometrics around week after extension is applied.

Price :

  • The price for the extension is start from IDR 800.000 (normal service, 14 working days process).
  • If you are in need of express procedure, you can choose 5, or even 3 working days process (for the detail of this service, please contact us throught whatsapp or email).
