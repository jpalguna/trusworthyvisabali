B211A Visa (Social Visa)

What is B211A visa?

  • This visa is applied to those who will visit Indonesia for the purpose of official government activities, tourism, social and cultural activities, family visit activities, or transit. This Visa allows foreigner to stay in Indonesia up to 180 days.
  • When the foreigner arrived in Indonesia, The Officer at the airport will give the stamp for 60 days, If the foreigners wants to stay until 180 days, they can extend the visa until 2 times, every extension will get 60 days.

Document requirements for apply B221A visa

  • Passport with at least 12 months validity for a single-entry visitor visa application with a validity period of 180 days.
  • A proof of Bank Statement with minimum balance at least 2,000 USD or equivalent.
  • Photograph Half Body with Blue, Red or White Background.
  • Vaccine Certificate Minimum 2 doses.

How to apply B211A visa

  • Click the link to connect with our whatsapp or email.
  • Choose the services that you want to take or you can discuss with our staff on whatsapp or email.
  • Send all of the document of requirements by whatsapp or email.
  • We will give you the Invoice to make the payment. After you make the payment, we will start the process of your Visa based on the services that you choose.
  • After the E- Visa issued, we will send it through your whatsapp or email.

Note :

  • Please kindly wait for the process until the E-Visa issued and do not enter Indonesia without the E- Visa.
  • The process of the E-Visa is based on the working days of Indonesian Government, so, it will not Include weekend and public holiday.
  • When the E-Visa already issued, please kindly enter Indonesia before 90 days since the E-Visa issued or the visa will be canceled automatically.
  • After you arrived in Indonesia, please kindly send the sticker or stamp of arrival through our whatsapp or email, so, we can remind you later for the extension.
